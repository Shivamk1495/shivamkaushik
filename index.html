Shivam Kaushik

My name is Shivam Kaushik, and I am a Researcher at the University of Florida focused on the intersection of Behavior and Neurobiology. My current work involves developing therapeutic strategies for Neurodegenerative Diseases by studying the molecular mechanisms of neurotoxicity. Through my academic journey across India and the USA, I have developed a deep expertise in Drosophila Genetics, using this powerful model to map circuits responsible for feeding, foraging, and Dopamine-Dependent Plasticity.
